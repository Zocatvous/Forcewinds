Makashi
Ysalamiri Style
Kohun Style


The Way of the Ysalamiri relies on careful and controlled strikes. It is excellent in duels against other lightsaber stylists.


Form Bonus: Accuracy, Defence and Damage Bonus.


* All (*) techniques cannot be performed without a curved disruptor hilt.
* All Lightsaber combatants who roll perception to determine that this style is being used must also have at least one level in Makashi.


Advantage: Striking from advantage in makashi allows you to reroll your Accuracy die pool for any offense and take the better of the two rolls.


Disadvantage: When in disadvantage in Makashi, opponents who successfully land strikes that do Aitizen damage against you gain advantage.
